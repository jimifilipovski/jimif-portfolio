Amon Tobin

Amon Tobin iSam World Tour was a large scale visual for a live concert. It was a collaboration between Leviathan and V-Squared Labs. I was responsible for the animation to the song "Piece of Paper".

The bulk of my work came to adding the finishing layers of the 3D-animation. Working closely with the animator Dave Breuder and Art Director Sam G. I added some and light flickers as well as some scan lines and other goodies.
