About

People often ask me what kind of designer I am and I have to say it’s a difficult question to answer. The better question is what type of designer am I not? I approach everything in life through design, I love solving everyday problems through a design-conscious process. Everything is design. Unraveling objects with this view gives a full circle approach to life.

To be more specific I’m an animator, director, filmmaker, photographer, leather worker, web designer, guitar playing, french learning, book reading, coffee drinking human being.
