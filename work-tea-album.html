The Niche

The Eternal Affect approached me to design their album "In Balance with This Life, This Death." I had full creative freedom.

I took the traditional renderings of the human anatomy and mixed them with pop art colors to create a more surreal experience.

We also held a dinner for the first 8 creatives. Put on by our own Kara Dykert and Events by Willow. It was one thing to feature great talent in Chicago, but even better when you can get them all in the same room with each other.
